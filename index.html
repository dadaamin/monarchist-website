Heavy riffs, melodic hooks, clean baritone vocals - this is probably the most suitable way to describe the music of German quartet MONARCHIST. Going back to the pioneers of metal music and putting a modern melodic spin on it by injecting influences from the broad spectrum of today's music, all while striving innovation, makes MONARCHIST a true 2020's heavy rock band. Most songs deal with the mannerisms and flaws of mankind, whether in form of social commentary, or self-reflection. For the new generation of fans of Black Sabbath, Metallica, Ghost, Iron Maiden, Rammstein, and more.
